Privacy Policy

Detailed clinical protocols on how AI-Powered Predictive Health Tracker manages, encrypts, and secures your systemic health markers.

1. Digital Patient Identity

To access the **AI-Powered Predictive Health Tracker**, we utilize secure Google OAuth 2.0. We only collect your primary identity markers to associate diagnostic assessments with your unique Clinical ID.

2. Neural Data Encryption

All health markers (Resting HR, Sleep Depth, PEM indices) are stored in an encrypted SQLite registry. Data transmission between your client and our LSTM neural core is protected via industry-standard TLS 1.3 protocols.

3. Clinical HIPAA Alignment

While this platform is a predictive wellness tool, we adhere to the core principles of the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA). We ensure your systemic diagnostics are never shared with third-party aggregators without explicit patient consent.

4. Forensic Audit Logs

We maintain record of every diagnostic audit performed. This ensures that you have a consistent medical history that can be verified by healthcare professionals at your discretion.
